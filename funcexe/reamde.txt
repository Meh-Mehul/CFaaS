Design idea behind the Function Executor (Funcexe):


[As is in my every recent project] The workermanager schedules a free worker.
The worker (after being split as a thread) runs a systemd-like process at 
initialization and sets up a unix socket server to listen for fd transfers.

The main scheduler finds a free worker and schedules another thread (never joined),
which does the worker of transferring the fd as well handling the manager-side state
of the worker.

In this way, only a single program handles the workers, their startup and their
execution.
